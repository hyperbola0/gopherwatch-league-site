Player History

Joining UMN Esports in 2017, Ryan "BOON1E" Buendorf was with the club for 3 1/2 years. In this time, BOON1E competed in the Tespa collegiate Overwatch tournament for 3 years for multiple teams. BOON1E was elected club president in 2019. After the shut down of Tespa due to Covid-19, BOON1E established the Gopherwatch League so players from the University of Minnesota and other colleges could still play semi-competitively. To this day, BOON1E remains the league commissioner.
